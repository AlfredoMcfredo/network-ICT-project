WHAT IS A COMPUTER NETWORK?

Most computers are connected together nowadays to form a network. This can be a basic network of only a few computers such as a school or home network (often connected to share resources such as printers and software), or it can be a large network such as the internet, which effectively allows any computer connected to it to communicate with any other computer similarly connected.
